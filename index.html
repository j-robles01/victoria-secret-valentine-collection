The Campaign

A Night Made for
Staying In

There's a particular kind of magic that lives in the quiet hours — candlelight catching the gleam of satin, the softness of a robe pulled close, the warmth of a night without plans. This Valentine's Day, we're celebrating the ritual of the sleepover: slow mornings, tender evenings, and everything in between.

"Dressed in silk and nothing else — this is the only Valentine's plan you need."

The Valentine's Sleepover Collection was designed for women who know that romance lives in the details. From the barely-there weight of our signature satin to the enveloping warmth of our plush robes, every piece invites you to slow down, feel beautiful, and lean into the luxurious art of doing absolutely nothing — with someone you love, or entirely on your own.

Playful lace at the hem. Soft modal that moves with you. Colors that blush like candlelight — rose, cream, deep bordeaux, and dusty blush. This collection isn't about performance. It's about presence.
